LANNA
The construction of the temple started in the 14th century, when King planned to bury the ashes of his father there. After 10 years of building time it was left unfinished, later to be continued after the death of the king by his widow. Probably due to stability problems it took until the mid-15th century to be finished during the reign of king . It was then 82 m high and had a base diameter of 54 m, at that time the largest building of all.
